How To Play

In this game your goal is to block as many projectiles as you can while not getting hit yourself. To do this you have a magic shield of which you can "throw" or hold to block the rainbow fire. Throwing the shield will hold it in place hovering farther in the direction thrown allowing the blocking of far away projectiles.Each block grants you a point and each time you are shot it removes a hit point. If your hit points reaches 0 you will break down and get a game over.

Story

You play as ShieldBot a machine built long ago in a past so far it's belived to be myth. The robot wanders the world looking for it's lost treasure. Using a magical shield called Regulus he protects all those in danger he comes across even if they usually see him as a threat. He doesn't know but the treasure he has been looking for this whole time is Regulus, just don't tell him. Right now he finds himself in a situation where a bunch of random, seemingly non physical things are shooting at him. What are they? Why are they breaking laws of physics? Well why not play and find out?
